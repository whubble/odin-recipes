GUMBO

Description

This dish is a Cajun specialty goin back to the first Acadians to settle in Louisiana. It is made of a rich roux and served over rice with chicken and sausage.

It is a staple dish during the cold weather months. It's roux broth warms the bones and hearty meat keeps you full for those long winter hibernations.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Sausage
  • Rice
  • Roux
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Cajun seasoning

Steps

  1. Add 1 gollon of water to a large stock pot
  2. Bring to a boil and add half a jar of roux
  3. Add one diced onion and chopped celery
  4. Add deboned raw chicken and 2 links of sausage
  5. Boil until chicken is fully cooked
  6. Serve in a bowl over cooked rice
